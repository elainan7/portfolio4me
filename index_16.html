About Me

Hi, I'm Elaina Niederer — a recent summa cum laude graduate from Seattle University with a degree in Communication and Media. I'm at the beginning of my career and genuinely excited about it.

What draws me to marketing is the space where creativity and strategy overlap. I like asking why something works, not just making it look good. I've had the chance to work on campaigns, social content, research projects, and writing across a range of contexts — and each one has taught me something about how people actually respond to communication.

I'm building toward a career where storytelling and data inform each other — finding the work that has real purpose behind it.
