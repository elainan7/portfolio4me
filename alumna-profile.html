About This Project

This project demonstrates full end-to-end editorial ownership — from reaching out and conducting the interview, to shaping the narrative, writing the copy, and designing the final layout for the department's quarterly publication.

The alumna profile format requires balancing a subject's personal voice with an institutional publication's tone, while making the piece genuinely readable and compelling for a broader audience.

Skills Demonstrated

Primary source interview — conducting and shaping the conversation
Long-form narrative writing for a department publication
Editorial layout design within a newsletter format
Institutional voice — balancing personal story with brand tone
Managing a complete editorial workflow independently
