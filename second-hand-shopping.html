The Three Shopper Personas

Persona 01

The Conscious Consumer

Motivated primarily by sustainability and environmental values. Views secondhand shopping as an ethical choice and actively seeks out thrift as a lifestyle, not just a budget strategy.

Persona 02

The Budget Shopper

Driven by financial practicality. Secondhand shopping is a cost-effective solution — less ideologically motivated, more outcome-focused. Value and price point are the primary decision drivers.

Persona 03

The Trend Seeker

Drawn to the uniqueness and vintage appeal of secondhand finds. Fashion-forward and identity-conscious — thrifting is a way to stand out, not just save money or reduce waste.

"The most interesting finding was how differently the same behavior — buying secondhand — was motivated across segments. Same action, completely different story behind it."

What I Learned

Survey Design

Building questions that yield clean, analyzable data — balancing specificity with accessibility for a college student audience.

Visual Storytelling

Translating raw data into visuals that communicate clearly — making findings accessible to an audience that hasn't seen the spreadsheets.

Consumer Segmentation

Identifying meaningful patterns in behavior and attitude that go beyond demographics — understanding the "why" behind purchasing decisions.
