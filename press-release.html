For Immediate Release

Shiseido Announces Partnership with Michelle Yeoh, Pedro Pascal, and Anne Hathaway for "Shiseido Blooms" Campaign

Initiative to Support Local Florists and Flower Farms Across the U.S.

New York, NY — May 11, 2023

Shiseido Company, Limited ("Shiseido") is excited to unveil its latest campaign, Shiseido Blooms, set to launch on June 28, 2023. The campaign will feature a star-studded video series with global icons Michelle Yeoh, Pedro Pascal, and Anne Hathaway. Shiseido Blooms will celebrate beauty, sustainability, and community by partnering with local florists and flower farms across the United States.

As part of the initiative, customers who purchase Shiseido products starting on June 28 will receive a flower seed native to their state, along with a personalized message encouraging them to let their beauty bloom, just like a flower. This gesture highlights Shiseido's ongoing commitment to sustainability and nurturing local communities.

"We are thrilled to partner with local flower suppliers and farms across the U.S. for this campaign. At Shiseido, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do, and Shiseido Blooms represents our dedication to supporting local businesses while promoting the timeless message that beauty must be nurtured to truly flourish."

— Masahiko Uotani, CEO, Shiseido Company, Limited

The Shiseido Blooms campaign also underscores Shiseido's broader mission to promote beauty and wellness through innovation and sustainability. The company's philosophy emphasizes the nurturing of beauty from within, inspiring individuals to feel confident in their own skin while protecting the world's natural beauty.

Michelle Yeoh will travel to Shiseido's Japan Factory in Nasu to film the campaign, adding her personal touch to the project.

"I am proud to be part of Shiseido Blooms, a campaign that connects beauty, nature, and community. It's a reminder that beauty is about care and connection, and that together, we can make a positive impact on the world around us."

— Michelle Yeoh, Campaign Partner

"We're excited to bring Shiseido Blooms to the U.S. The campaign reflects Shiseido's belief that true beauty comes from within and is enhanced by self-care, all while supporting local communities and small businesses."

— Marc Rey, CEO, Shiseido Americas

Shiseido Blooms marks a significant milestone in Shiseido's continued dedication to sustainability, culture, and beauty. The company invites customers across the U.S. to join the movement and experience the joy of nurturing beauty in every form.

For more information about Shiseido and Shiseido Blooms, please visit www.shiseidogroup.com.
