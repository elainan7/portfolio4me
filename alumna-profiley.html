About This Project

This project involved the full editorial pipeline — reaching out to Dana, preparing and conducting the interview, shaping the narrative into a feature article, and designing the final newsletter layout for the department's Spring 2024 publication.

It required balancing Dana's personal voice with the institution's tone, while making the piece genuinely engaging for a broader academic audience.
