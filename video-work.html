Pike Place Market Foundation

Raluca — A Memory of the Market

The hook for this piece came from a single question: what specific memory does this place hold for you? By asking Raluca to go beyond general support and recall something personal, I found the emotional core of the story. As creative director, I developed the interview questions, led the filming strategy, handled audio editing, and assembled the final piece from footage — building a narrative around memory, place, and what community spaces actually mean to people.
