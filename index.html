01 — About

Where ideas
meet intention

I recently graduated summa cum laude from Seattle University with a degree in Communication and Media, spending the past few years figuring out how to turn ideas into something people actually connect with.

I've worked across marketing, social media, and community-focused projects — leading teams, building campaigns, and creating content that feels intentional. I like the balance: the creative side of storytelling, and the strategic side of understanding what works and why.

At the core of it, I'm interested in communication that feels real. Work that resonates, that people actually engage with, and that serves a purpose beyond just being seen.
